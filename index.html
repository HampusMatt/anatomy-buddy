Welcome to the home for students of Anatomy

About us

Anatomy-Buddy is specifically designed as a hub for students of anatomy. Here you can find information about muscles and their functions, about joints and all your nerves!

How does this site work?

This website will provide you with tabels overviewing specific joints; the muscles that are associated with it and the origins, insertions, functions, and nerve innervations of said muscles. Click on "Get to studying", pick a joint, and Viola! All the information you could need all in one handy place.
